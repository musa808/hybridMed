About Our Hybrid Medical Consultancy System

The Hybrid Medical Consultancy System is a modern digital health platform designed to support both physical and remote healthcare services.

Our Mission

To improve healthcare delivery by integrating patient management, doctor coordination, appointment scheduling, medical records, and consultation services into one unified system.

System Features

  • ✔ Patient Registration & Management
  • ✔ Doctor Profile Management
  • ✔ Appointment Scheduling
  • ✔ Consultation Tracking
  • ✔ Digital Medical Records
  • ✔ Secure User Authentication

Why Hybrid?

The system supports both in-person and virtual consultations, allowing healthcare providers to serve patients more efficiently while improving accessibility and patient care quality.

Developed for modern healthcare delivery.
